1. Siruba is known for its industrial and commercial sewing machines.

  2. Their machines are designed for heavy-duty use in garment manufacturing, textile production, and other industrial sewing applications.

  3. Siruba machines typically offer high-speed stitching, durable construction, and versatility to handle different types of fabrics and sewing tasks.

  4. Common types of Siruba machines include lockstitch, overlock, interlock, and specialized machines for tasks like buttonholing and bartacking.

  5. Singer is a historic brand known for its domestic and industrial sewing machines.

  6. They offer a wide range of sewing machines catering to various users, from beginners to advanced sewers.

  7. Singer machines often feature user-friendly designs, built-in stitches, automatic threading, and adjustable stitch settings.

  8. They manufacture mechanical, computerized, heavy-duty, embroidery, quilting, and serger machines, among others.

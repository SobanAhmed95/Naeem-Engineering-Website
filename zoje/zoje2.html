1. Lockstitch Machines: These machines are designed for basic stitching tasks and are commonly used in garment manufacturing, upholstery, and other sewing applications.

  2. Overlock Machines: Also known as sergers, overlock machines are used for edging, hemming, and seaming fabric edges to prevent fraying. Zoje offers a range of overlock machines suitable for various sewing tasks.

  3. Interlock Machines: Interlock machines are used for sewing stretch fabrics and creating seams with good elasticity. They are commonly used in the production of sportswear, activewear, and lingerie.

  4. Coverstitch Machines: Coverstitch machines are used for hemming and decorative stitching on garments, particularly knit fabrics. They create a professional finish similar to what you would see on store-bought clothing.

  5. Specialized Machines: Zoje also manufactures specialized sewing machines for specific tasks such as buttonholing, button sewing, bartacking, and more.

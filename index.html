Faqs

Q: How to operate the sewing machine?

A: To operate the sewing machine, follow the user manual provided. Make sure to thread the needle properly and choose the right settings for your fabric.

Q: What should I do if the machine is not stitching properly?

A: Check the bobbin, needle, and thread tension. Ensure that the machine is threaded correctly. If issues persist, refer to the troubleshooting section in the manual or contact customer support.

Q: How often should I lubricate the machine?

A: Regular lubrication is essential for optimal performance. Refer to the maintenance schedule in the user manual for guidance on when and how to lubricate different parts of the machine.

Q: What types of fabrics can be used with the machine?

A: The machine can typically handle a wide range of fabrics, including cotton, polyester, silk, and denim. However, it's always recommended to check the machine's specifications and adjust settings accordingly for different fabric types.

Q: Can the machine be used for embroidery?

A: Some garments machines come with embroidery features or attachments that allow for embroidery work. Check the machine's capabilities and available accessories for embroidery options.
